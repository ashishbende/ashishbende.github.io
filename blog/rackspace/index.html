Summer internship at Rackspace

I was an intern at Rackspace during summer 2016. In a short span developed an end-to-end data connector for managed MongoDB/Elasticsearch ecosystem.

A data connector which consumes real-time Twitter feeds and store it in either MongoDB or Elasticsearch instance. Also coded a pre-defined Kibana dashboard template, and POC product tour to enhance user experience.
